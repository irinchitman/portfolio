The Deli Club F&B

Brand identity, website design and packaging design for a blog that captures unusual food stories from kitchens, homes, cafes and markets around the globe.

Brand Personality

The Deli Club is warm, dreamy, and graceful.

The Deli Club approaches new culture and stories with warmth, gentleness and openess.

She is sometimes stuck in a fantasy, dreaming and pondering about the little wonders of the world and sometimes digressing.

Being adventurous doesn't mean the she is ragged. She travels and explores with grace and style.
