Life's Good Here Retail

Marketing campaign to strengthten brand awareness for the Emporium Department Store.

Insights

In Bangkok, the location of the department store, there is an increase in interest and support for small businesses and locally made goods.

Retail customers are looking for unique and exclusive experiences and products.

Response

Step 1 We bring together 30 Sukhumvit-based shops to co-create one-of-a-kind goodies and experiences inspired by the neighborhood. The Emporium Department Store too is located in Sukhumvit neighborhood.

Secondhand books selected from Sukhumvit's Dasa Book Cafe by notable names in the local creative industry

Handkerchief screenprinted with Thai 90s-2000s pop song lyrics designed and manufactured by Onion, a local curated fashion store and the Cassette Music Bar, a popular 90s songs bar

Tom Yum Sorbet, a special menu concocted by Saew, a local noodle shop, and Jaiyen, a local ice cream stall.

Step 2 We design a pop-up shop within the department store to house the goodies.

Sukhumvit experts hosted daily workshops within the space.

Customers can add and share their favorite Sukhumvit stores on the communual neighborhood map.

Step 3 We take over the media surrounding the department store.

Step 4 We engage the neighborhood, by placing the one-of-a-kind co-created products in the shops of their Sukhumvit creators.
