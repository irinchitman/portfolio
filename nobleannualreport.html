Simple: Noble Annual Report 2016 Publishing

A booklet that conveys and assures the values of a Noble Development Group LLC to the shareholders.

Insights

Noble Development Group LLC is a leading developer in Thailand with almost 50 residential projects in Thailand, a total gross value of 900 million.

Noble takes pride in creating homes that are " uniquely modern and iconic, go above and beyond our boundary to reach the exciting new world". Noble creates simple modern design with a focus on high quality materials.

Response

To capture Noble's modernity, we create an accordion printless booklet titled "Simple is" as a play on the phrase "Simple is better." Accordian booklet doesn't require any sewing and is the simplest form of bookbinding. It forms also creates nooks of light and shadow like Noble buildings. We hot pressed simple graphics and phrases on to a special paper PACHICA. The paper turns translucent and waxy when hotpressed.

The accordion booklet accompanies Noble's annual report.
