Surin Pitsuwan Foundation NPO

Brand identity for a foundation aimed to solve and overcome challenges in education, diplomacy and human security in ASEAN.

Insight

Surin Pitsuwan had deep ties to his religious background. His family ran a madrasah, a school for Islamic religious instruction.

The mission of the foundation is to inspire citizens to rise above their differences and bring prosperity to the region.

Response

We create an identity that represents the spark for a better future. The star symbol borrows its geometric shape from symetrical and geometric Isamic art.
