Fedrigoni 100.5Publishing

Paper sample booklet content and visual design for Fedrigoni, an Italian paper manufacturer.

Insights

Paper sample booklets tend to get stowed away inside cabinets. Designers only pulled them out to be used as printing references toward the end of the process.

Response

We want to create a booklet that will not get stow away. We want to create a booklet that sits on designers' desks and is used throughout the design process.

To achieve this goal, we decided to create a booklet that offers some basic graphic design and typography tips and facts designers can look up during the design processs on top of showcasing the different printing techniques on the different Fedrigoni paper.
