Out of Office Retail

Summer marketing campaign to increase customer engagement for the Emporium Department Store.

Insights

Retail customers are looking for new experiences and products.

All office workers look forward to summer vacation, but not all can afford to take an actual vacation.

Response

Step 1 We turn vacation into a state of mind and create a brand and a product line that offer escapes for office workers.

Photography: Patcha Kitchaicharoen

Step 2 We design a pop-up shop that aims to drive engagement. The shop includes social media friendly activities.

He's playing Minesweeper.

Customers can clock-in and clock out at time clock machine. Our time cards are labeled with special titles.

The dot matrix machine prints out the customers' profiles. A digital version of the same print can be shared on social media.

The catering for the opening also had a matching office theme.
