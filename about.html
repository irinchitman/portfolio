Bio

Irin is a designer who specializes in conceptualizing and visualizing the brandings for cafes, restaurants, events and direct-to-consumer products.

Currently, Irin is a candidate for the Master of Management in Hospitality at Cornell University. She moonlights as a writer (about food) and as an entrepreneur selling exotic juice.

Previously, Irin worked as a senior designer at Farmgroup, a Bangkok-based design consultancy studio.

Besides working with F&B clients, Irin also had the pleasure of working with Apple, Surin Pitsuwan Foundation, Redesign Health, Aula Education, Teach for Thailand among others.
