Cup of Joie Retail

Brand identity for a fuss-free and waste-free alternative for menstrual care product.

Media Coverage: aday magazine
Official Instagram: cupsofjoie

Brand Statement

Joie wants to help women take control of their period and allow them to feel comfortable and confident in their body.

Brand Personality

Joie goes with the flow. We accept our body and understand the nature of it. We're mellow. But don't get us wrong. Although we're mellow, we are eloqient. We know what we deserve. We say it. We do what we want and maintain our optimistic free-spirit. With this we hope to inspire other women to be like us.

Brand Identity

To capture Joei's personality, we create a mark for Joie that is an abstraction of a kite, a heart and a woman's upper thigh.

Waste-free Packaging

Joie partners with Second Chance Bangkok to turn leftover clothings into menstrual cup pouches. We select fabrics that matches the color scheme of the brand.

Joei's delivery packagings are made out of 100% recycled paper, printed in soy ink, and composed of the eco-friendly dried water hyacith instead of the harmful bubble wrap.

Photography Art Direction

To capture Joie's personality, we advised that Joie includes photos that are 80% active/20% resting, 80% outdoor/20% in door, and 80% indulgent/20% practical.
